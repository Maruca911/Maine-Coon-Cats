Basic Cat Commands: Training Your Cat Step by Step

Training your cat can be a rewarding experience that strengthens your bond. This guide will help you teach your cat basic commands using positive reinforcement techniques.

Getting Started with Training

Before you begin training, you'll need:

  • High-value treats your cat loves
  • A quiet, distraction-free environment
  • Patience and consistency
  • Short training sessions (5-10 minutes)

Basic Commands

Sit

  1. Hold a treat above your cat's head
  2. Move it slowly backward
  3. When their bottom touches the ground, say "sit"
  4. Immediately reward with the treat

Come

  1. Start with your cat nearby
  2. Say their name and "come"
  3. Show them a treat
  4. Reward when they approach

Stay

  1. Have your cat sit first
  2. Show your palm and say "stay"
  3. Take a small step back
  4. Reward if they remain in place

Training Tips

  • Keep sessions short and positive
  • Use consistent commands and hand signals
  • Gradually increase difficulty
  • End on a successful note
  • Practice regularly

Common Challenges

Lack of Interest

  • Try different treats
  • Choose a better time of day
  • Make training more engaging

Distractions

  • Train in a quiet room
  • Remove other pets
  • Close windows and doors
