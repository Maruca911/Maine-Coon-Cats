FAQs About Cat Dental Products

What's the best way to introduce dental products to my cat?

Start with treats and toys, then gradually introduce brushing with positive reinforcement.

Are water additives safe for cats?

Yes, when used as directed, water additives are safe and effective for maintaining dental health.

How often should I replace my cat's toothbrush?

Replace the toothbrush every 3-4 months or when the bristles show signs of wear.
