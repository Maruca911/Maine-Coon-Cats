Easy Homemade Recipes

Chicken & Rice Delight

A simple, nutritious recipe perfect for your cat.

20 min prep 30 min cook

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/4 cup carrots, steamed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp taurine supplement

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken breast thoroughly
  2. Steam carrots until soft
  3. Mix all ingredients together
  4. Add taurine supplement
  5. Serve at room temperature

Fish & Vegetable Medley

A healthy recipe rich in omega-3 fatty acids.

15 min prep 25 min cook

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked salmon
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/4 cup carrots
  • 1 tbsp fish oil
  • 1/4 tsp taurine supplement

Instructions:

  1. Cook salmon thoroughly
  2. Steam vegetables
  3. Mix ingredients together
  4. Add supplements
  5. Serve at room temperature

Storage & Safety Guidelines

Proper Storage

Store homemade food in airtight containers in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, or freeze for up to 2 months.

Clean Preparation

Always use clean utensils and surfaces when preparing cat food to prevent contamination.

Meal Timing

Serve food at room temperature and remove any uneaten portions after 30 minutes.

Transitioning to Homemade Food

Day 1-3

Mix 25% homemade food with 75% regular food

Day 4-6

Increase to 50% homemade food

Day 7-10

Transition to 100% homemade food

Frequently Asked Questions

How much homemade food should I feed my cat?

Adult cats typically need 2-4% of their body weight in food per day. For example, a 10-pound cat would need 3.2-6.4 ounces of food daily.

Do I need to add supplements?

Yes, it's important to add taurine and other essential supplements to ensure complete nutrition. Consult your veterinarian for specific recommendations.

Can I use raw ingredients?

While some owners feed raw food, it's safer to cook ingredients thoroughly to prevent bacterial infections. Always handle raw meat with care.
