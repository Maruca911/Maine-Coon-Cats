Coat Care

Regular Brushing

Brush 2-3 times weekly with a stainless steel comb and slicker brush. Focus on areas prone to matting.

Seasonal Care

Increase brushing during shedding seasons. Use an undercoat rake for thick winter coats.

Coat Conditioner

Use a quality leave-in conditioner to prevent tangles and maintain coat health.

Essential Grooming Tools

  • Stainless steel comb
  • Slicker brush
  • Undercoat rake
  • Nail clippers
  • Ear cleaning solution
  • Cat-safe shampoo

Bathing Guide

Preparation

Brush thoroughly before bathing to remove loose hair and prevent matting.

Water Temperature

Use lukewarm water (about 38°C/100°F) for comfort.

Drying

Use a high-velocity dryer on low setting to prevent matting.

Nail Care

Trimming Frequency

Trim nails every 2-3 weeks to prevent overgrowth and splitting.

Proper Technique

Cut only the clear part of the nail, avoiding the quick. Use sharp, cat-specific clippers.

Scratching Posts

Provide multiple scratching surfaces to help maintain nail health naturally.

Ear & Eye Care

Ear Cleaning

  • Clean ears weekly with vet-approved solution
  • Check for signs of infection (redness, odor)
  • Never use cotton swabs inside the ear canal

Eye Care

  • Wipe eyes daily with damp cloth
  • Check for excessive tearing or discharge
  • Consult vet for persistent eye issues
